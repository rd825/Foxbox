CLASS AND AFFORDABILITY ROLLED INTO ONE

Foxbox curates gourmet, nonperishable food items sourced in the Washington DC area into boxes. We're really proud to support local eateries and food startups that are redefining the culinary landscape. We are unabashed foodies and we want to share our love of food with you. Let's discover DC together!

PRICING

Our target market is college students who want food just like (or even better than) what Mom used to make. To that end, we have decided to price our Foxboxes at $20/box. That's less than a typical dinner out on the town in DC! Impress your friends with amazing snacks when they come over for parties. Cook incredible meals on date night. Foxbox is committed to giving you the most amazing food experience for as cheap as possible.

DELIVERIES

We will hand-deliver our boxes right to your door. We have an unbelievable commitment to customer service. That means ensuring that every box is treated with the care it deserves as it travels from origin to destination. Moreover, we want to get to know our customers. By delivering our boxes personally, Team Foxbox can establish a personal relationship and get the feedback we need to grow as a company!
